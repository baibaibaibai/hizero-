About HIZERO

Founded in 2013, HIZERO Technologies CO.,LTD specializes in innovative cleaning technology. Inspired by bionics, we created the world’s first dual-use and self-cleanable floor cleaner, HIZERO. It integrates the current dry cleaning tools (e.g.brooms and vacuum cleaners) and wet cleaning tools (e.g.damp paper towels and mops) into one. Established by a group of engineers and designers, we pursue a perfect balance of aesthetics and functionality. The upgraded generations of HIZERO are already under development.

Originated from the nature, HIZERO will make your life simpler.
